Get a taste of farm fresh goods in the heart of the city

Harvest Hustle is a working farm located in Toronto's Downtown Queen station neighbourhood. Customers to our 7.5 acre property will get a first hand taste of farm products. The property features flowers, vegetables, herb gardens and dairy as well as wooded areas and ponds connected to the city's rich ravine system. We founded Harvest Hustle in the month of May in the year 1989, not quite 3 years later, our team reached a significant milestone - the $1million sales mark. We believe our accomplishments are a result of hard work, team spirit, and a determination to constantly improve upon our products.
